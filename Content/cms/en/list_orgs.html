Are you active in a political-interest organization? Do you want to include your organization's viewpoint in a centrally-located forum that will reach the eyes of millions?

The average citizen can have difficulty interpreting purely political issues, and Kamu seeks to provide expert opinions on a variety of subjects. We want organizations of all political leanings — from left-to-right — to bring focus to major issues addressed within the Finnish parliament.

Kamu's member organizations will be provided the chance to comment on legislative proposals and simplify language for the citizens of Finland to understand.

Please contact us to join the revolution!
